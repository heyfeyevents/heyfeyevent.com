our services

Engagement Event

The goal of event engagement is to create an immersive experience that encourages attendees to get involved and have a good time.

Nikkah Event

Nikkah decorations allow the couple to express their personalities and preferences. The choice of colors, flowers, and decor elements reflects their unique style, making the ceremony a true representation of their love.

Mayon Event

The Mayon event is a pre-wedding ceremony in Pakistan that marks the start of wedding festivities. It takes place a few days or a week before the wedding. The Mayon event is characterized by vibrant colors and is often held in a cozy setting.

Mehndi Event

A mehndi party is a pre-wedding celebration. During this event, the bride has a red-orange mehndi applied to her palms, the back of her hands, and her feet. "The family and friends of the bride adorn her with this orange.

Barat Event

The Barat ceremony marks the arrival of the groom and his family to the wedding venue, accompanied by music, dancing, and cheerful celebrations.

Walima Event

A Walima is a traditional Muslim wedding reception that takes place after the marriage ceremon.It is a significant event in Islamic culture and is usually a large celebration with family, friends, and relatives.

Qawali Night

A Qawwali night is an event that features Qawwali music, a popular music genre in South Asia and North India. Qawwali nights are often held at weddings and other gatherings, and are enjoyed by people of all ages.

Bridal Shower

A bridal shower is a pre-wedding celebration that honors the bride-to-be with gifts, congratulations, and good wishes. It's a chance for the bride's loved ones to show their support and celebrate with her.

Corporate Event

A corporate event is a business-sponsored event that can be about a company's employees, clients, or both. Corporate events can have many purposes, including: Educating, Celebrating, Motivating, Networking, Promoting, Socializing.

product photography

Photography is the art, application, and practice of creating images by recording light, either electronically by means of an image sensor, or chemically by means of a light-sensitive material.

Birthday event

A birthday event is a celebration of a person's birth anniversary. It's a time to honor the person, acknowledge their growth, and express gratitude.

Decore

A decoration is anything used to make something more attractive or festive. Decoration is also a type of honor.
